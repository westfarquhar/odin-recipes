Chicken is delicious!


Chicken is delicious. Especially fried.. Many countries around the world enjoy frying and eating the bird. Gentically modified for maximum breast size. coated in egg and breadcrumbs and spices. from Karaage in the land of the rising sun to Colonel Sanders in the Land of the white cloud. It is universially loved and appreciated around the world. For the DIY crowd, why not make your own? Of course this not a proper recipe so dont use this, please go to All Recipes for proper information.


Ingredients

  • chicken
  • breadcrumbs or panko
  • butter milk
  • salt
  • cooking oil
  • spices of your choosing

Preparation

  1. buy a baby chicken
  2. raise baby chicken if hen use for eggs until old
  3. hug and apologize to chicken
  4. ask a friend to kill chicken
  5. cry and thank chicken for its service
  6. pluck and gut chicken remove digestive system and head, save liver
  7. wash chicken in hot water
  8. break chicken down into parts
  9. soak chicken in salt brine with salt and orange peel
  10. paper towel chicken off
  11. coat chicken in egg
  12. roll chicken in flour, spices, and panko
  13. immerse chicken in hot oil cook until all bubbles go away

Enjoy!
