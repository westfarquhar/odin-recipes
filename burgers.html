Burgers are delicious!


For the DIY crowd, why not make your own? Of course this not a proper recipe so dont use this, please go to All Recipes for proper information.


Ingredients

  • ground beef, after the trauma of the "chicken incident" perhaps its better to buy from the store, plus cows are much bigger than you.
  • double onion
  • double garlic
  • egg
  • panko or breadcrumbs
  • seasonings including chicken stock cubes, paprika, garlic and onion powder, black pepper, add your own flair
  • chedder or jalapeno cheese
  • romaine lettuce
  • cherry tomatoes
  • mushrooms
  • pickle
  • mayo, ketchup, mustard
  • quality burger buns

Preparation

  • chop garlic and onion, prep lettuce, mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes
  • use half of the garlic and onions aad saute the sliced mushrooms, set aside
  • mix the rest of the garlic and onions with egg and panko and seasonings into the ground beef
  • press beef into patties, make them extra large as they will shrink
  • fry or grill patties on medium low heat until cooked through, melt cheese on the patties at the last minute
  • toast burger buns

Burger stack assembly

  1. start with bottom bun
  2. sauces on the bottom
  3. fresh veggies next the lettuce and the tomatoes
  4. burger patty with cheese
  5. add pickles
  6. sauce on the top bun
  7. cap it, use a burger sword to hold it all together

Enjoy!
